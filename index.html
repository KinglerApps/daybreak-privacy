Privacy Policy for Daybreak

Effective date: June 1, 2026

Daybreak ("the app," "we," "us") is a focus and screen-time app. This policy explains what data the app accesses, why, and how it is handled. Our core principle is simple: your personal usage data stays on your device.

Data We Collect

Daybreak does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information to us or to third parties. The app does not have user accounts, and it does not upload your app-usage history, schedules, or settings to any server. All of that information remains on your device.

The one exception is anonymous crash reports — see "Crash Reporting" below.

Permissions We Use and Why

  • Usage Access: Daybreak reads which app is currently in the foreground so it can detect when you open an app you've chosen to block during a scheduled window. This information is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted off it.
  • Accessibility Service: Daybreak uses the Accessibility Service to enforce blocking — for example, to display the block/shield screen over a distracting app. It is used solely for this blocking function and does not read, store, or share the content of your screen.
  • Notifications: Daybreak sends notifications to inform you about active blocking windows and related app status. These are generated locally on your device.

Crash Reporting

To keep the app stable, Daybreak uses Firebase Crashlytics (a Google service) to collect anonymous crash and diagnostic data when the app stops unexpectedly. This may include the device model, operating system version, and technical details about the crash. It does not include the personal usage data described above, and it is not used to identify you. Firebase Crashlytics is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data. Daybreak contains no advertising and no analytics or tracking beyond the crash reporting described above.

Children's Privacy

Daybreak is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
